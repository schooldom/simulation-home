Simulation



Main Information

To spawn an object, click on your desired object. Then simply click anywhere on the screen to spawn it in.

When a collision occurs; one planet will be destroyed losing everything.

You can change many values of the different bodies, even the most fundamental ones such as the mass or volume. Experiment with see and see what chaos you can cause.

I would suggest starting with trying to make your own mini solar system with just one sun and one planet. Then see if you can add a moon without throwing the planet into the sun.
Good Luck :)






Fun Information

You can change the formulas used to calculate gravity in a drop down menu. This will allow you to see how doubling gravity or halving it would affect not only our solar system but one which you have made.

You will have a very basic solar sytem to start with just to demonstrate how the different objects will interact with eachother.

Hope you have fun :)
